Hats and Co

Welcome to Hats & Co.

The Hats and Co hat shop in Regent Street, Leamington Spa is owned by sisters Erica Smith and Pam Goddard. It is managed by the owners and dedicated staff who adore hats!

What we do

Hats

At Hats and Co, we hire out designer hats so that you will never see the same hat as yours at an event. Our favourite designers are: Richards, Vivien Sheriff, The Whiteley Hat Company, G.B.T. Hats, Gemma J.. As a company we only buy British Millinery.

Accessories

At Hats and Co, Erica and Pam source accessories from small individual designers from all over the world and only buy one or two of each piece to make it exclusive, be it jewellery, a bag, wrap or pashmina.
